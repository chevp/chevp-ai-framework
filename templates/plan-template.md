# Plan Template

> For concrete implementation tasks. Location: `context/plans/EXP-NNN-<description>.md`

```markdown
# EXP-NNN: <Plan Title>

## Goal
What should be achieved?

## Context
Why is this needed? What dependencies exist?

## Scope
- **IN Scope**: ...
- **NOT in Scope**: ...

## Steps
1. Step 1
2. Step 2
3. ...

## Affected Files
- `path/to/file.ext` — What will be changed

## Risks
- Risk 1 → Mitigation
- Risk 2 → Mitigation

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Criterion 1
- [ ] Criterion 2
```

## Naming Convention

- Open plans: `EXP-NNN-<description>.md` (three digits, sequential)
- Completed: `finished/EXP-FNNN-<description>.md`
